## How to structure a UX case study

- Problem statement Frame the user pain point, business context, and design goal.
- User research Collect signals through interviews, observations, audits, or desk research.
- Wireframes Explore structure and flows before polished screens take over.
- UI process Use components, hierarchy, interaction states, and responsive decisions.
- Testing Record feedback, usability notes, and iterations that changed the solution.
- Final solution Present outcomes, trade-offs, next steps, and your interview story.
